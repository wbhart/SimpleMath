Binomial Coefficients

The integer coefficients of $(x + y)^n$ are called the binomial coefficients. We denote the coefficient of $x^{n - i}y^i$ by $\binom{n}{i}$. For $0 \leq i \leq n$ we have $$\binom{n}{i} = \binom{n}{n - i}.$$ We have $(x + y)^n = (y + x)^n$. But the coefficient of $x^{n - i}y^i$ is $\binom{n}{i}$ on the left and $\binom{n}{n - i}$ on the right.
